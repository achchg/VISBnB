Match buttons

The match buttons show the matches in current stage, representing matches in sequence.

In each stage, there are supposed to be 7 matches. If one team makes 4 wins, this team win this stage and get into the next stage.

The blue buttons with 'W' mean the Warriors win these matches.

Buttons in other color with 'L' mean the Warriors lose.

Gray buttons show canceled matches due to one team winning the stage already.

Yellow button will give you the overall average boxscore of this stage.
